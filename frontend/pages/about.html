Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about using SMPC marketplace

Is SMPC free to use?
Yes! Creating an account, browsing products, and listing items is completely free. We only charge a small fee when you successfully sell an item.
How do I know if a seller is trustworthy?
Check seller ratings, read reviews from previous buyers, and look for verification badges. Always communicate through our platform and trust your instincts.
What should I do if there's a problem with my purchase?
Contact our support team immediately. We have dispute resolution processes in place to help resolve issues between buyers and sellers fairly.
Can I return items I buy on SMPC?
Return policies vary by seller. Always check the item description and communicate with the seller about their return policy before making a purchase.
How do I get paid when I sell something?
Most transactions are completed with cash during in-person meetings. You can also use secure digital payment methods that both parties agree on.
